About Kawaii Tomodachi-desu

Kawaii Tomodachi-desu is an auto-generated dating sim featuring your Facebook friends. Built in 24 hours for Valentine's Day at TartanHacks, it aims to bring joy to friends who wish to date each other in a judgment-free virtual world.

Based off of popular Japanese dating simulations, Kawaii Tomodachi-desu creates a romantic adventure starring your very own Facebook friends. Each dating sim is tailored toward your friend: custom dialogue is generated by analyzing your friends' Facebook posts. Using the dissociated press algorithm, it chains together words in a believable yet nonsensical babble. Don't worry-- we don't store any personal information anywhere. All dialogue is generated on the fly!

Brought to you by Esther, Ivan, Jun, and Roger at TartanHacks 2016. Happy Valentine's Day!
